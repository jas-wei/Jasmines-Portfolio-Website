Nezha (ART360 Critique)

View on GitHub

A reimagined origin story of Nezha's 1979 movie. After a crane delivered Nezha to the Heavenly king, Nezha must find the Red Cloth and Golden Ring in order to defeat the Dragon King and stop the storms. This game is meant to mimic Chinese shadow play, as it is projected onto a paper-cut background, making for a seamless blend between the story's traditional origin and the modern medium.
